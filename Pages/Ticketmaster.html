Ticketmaster

This is my first original game made which I made with unity. You play as a train conductor and have to inspect the passengers tickets to earn as high a score as possible. The game was inspired by Papers please by Lucas Pope and Preflight Panic by Ben "Yahtzee" Croshaw.
