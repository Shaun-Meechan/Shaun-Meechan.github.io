Privacy Policy for my dissertation project

This project collects certain user data to be used alongside the survey for my dissertation research project. The data that is collected is not personally identifiable. Instead after the game, you are given a GUID. This ID is used to associate the in-game data with your survey answers. This cannot be used to personally identify you. You are free to leave the project at any time and have your data deleted. To request your data to be deleted please email 1801006@uad.ac.uk with the subject "Dissertation deletion request" and include your GUID in the body. You can get your GUID when you finish the game.
