AI

This project was created for my module CMP304 at university. This module looks at AI technologies which are being used or could be used in the future to enhance intelligent game systems.

This involves looking at traditional AI systems such as pathfinding, rule based system, finite state machines etc. Then researching technology such as ML and neural networks.

The assesment for this project was broken into two parts. One part for a comparision of two AI techniques/technologies and the other for ML based emotion recognition.

The technologies that I chose were RL and GA. To test these I created a game where the AI had to throw a ball into a number of cups (think of beer pong).
